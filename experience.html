Experience

A bit about my experiences.

What have i done?

Or, what have i not done?

I came from Chile to Sweden with my mother in a cold winter of '87. Her goal was to create a better future in a free country.

I grew up in a small town mostly doing small town stuff. In high school i went to a Media Program to study Photography, Movie making and some Interactive Design.

But my life changed a lot and many years later i was about to become a nurse for the elderly and that was when my hips gave up, only 27 years old. So i had to quit my studies and my job and rethink my life once again. And thank god that it happened, because without that i would of not have found CSS-Tricks, and through there Teamtreehouse.

So since April '13 i have been a Treehouse student, and i am currently enrolled in a Swedish Vocational University to become a Front-End JavaScript Developer, and now enrolled in Treehouse's brand new Tech degree as a beta tester.

My goal is to fill this page with work and experience, but first comes studies!

Much Love and thanks to the whole world for givind me and my mom a new chance.

"Happiness is someone to love, something to do, and something to hope for"
